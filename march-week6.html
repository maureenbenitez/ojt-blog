March

Coding

Today, we were introduced to new students who will be doing their OJT at URO. They are BS Economics students from CBEM and will also assist the office in handling records and documents.

Meanwhile, we continued our coding tasks. While Kent continued working on OTR, my task was to integrate the country API and store addresses in the database for future fetching. March

Coding

The exportation of certificates, such as the Good Moral Certificate, Graduation Certificate, Honorable Dismissal, and other certificates, has been completed.

Meanwhile, the fixing of OTR is still ongoing.
