April 14, 2025

April

Clerical Work

This week is actually Holy Week. However, we still have work until Wednesday morning. Today, Amir and I were the only ones who went to work—our other co-OJT members were absent since they’re heading back to their provinces.

Anyway, even with just the two of us, we were still able to do clerical work. Most of the employees were also present today, and we even held a flag ceremony.

We found out that the folders pulled by the Economics OJT students are now up to surnames starting with Q, so we still have a lot of scanning to do. We're currently at letter M, so we spent almost the whole day prepping the folders for scanning—removing unnecessary staples and documents that aren’t needed. We managed to finish four boxes of folders.

While we were doing that, Ma'am Gen also asked me to transfer all the scanned PDF files and their alpha list to her computer. Later on, we went with Ma'am Alex to the College of Education to assist her with some requests.

April 15, 2025

April

Coding

Today and tomorrow morning, we’re working from home. Our mentor and OJT coordinator gave us permission since our schedule falls on a coding day. Our progress and attendance are being tracked through our commits and pushes to our GitHub repository.

My task today was to handle validations for GWA and NSTP numbers. I made them nullable to account for cases like undergraduate students. This involved some migration work. I was able to finish the task and successfully committed and pushed it to GitHub.

After that, I continued debugging the issue with creating a student entry with courses that don’t have a course code, but I still haven’t figured it out yet.

April 16, 2025

April

Coding

We’re only working half a day today in observance of Holy Week. Since we're still working from home, we just continued our assigned tasks.

First, I fixed the display of the academic year on the View and Edit Student pages. Then, I reworked the sorting in the Students table, since there were errors after new columns were added. I also changed the redirection after verifying to go to the Students table instead of the View page. Lastly, I cleaned up the code by removing commented lines that are no longer needed.

All of these changes were committed and pushed to GitHub.
