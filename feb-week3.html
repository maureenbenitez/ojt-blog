February 20, 2025

February

Clerical Work

Despite the heavy rain, we went to work today for our scheduled clerical work. Since there was no internet, we focused on compiling and bundling of folders from last week's tasks. We scanned specific documents, renamed them, compiled the scanned documents, and separated them from their folders. We repeated this process throughout the day. We also learned that we will not have our launch this February because the system needs to be completed first. While it can already handle student entry, which is a major feature, they also request that the other features be implemented as well. Additionally, we had our URO IDs printed and laminated, making us feel like we’re officially part of the team now.

February 21, 2025

February

Clerical Work

After a long week of work, it's finally Friday! Classes were suspended, but we still reported to work. In the morning, I continued working on the alphabetical list of 2024 graduates, just as I did during last week's clerical work. I accomplished more than before since I was already familiar with the task. By the afternoon, we wrapped up as much as we could because work was dismissed early at 3:30 PM. The heavy rain had already caused flooding in some areas, so we were sent home early. The weather has been like this for the past few days, making work more exhausting. I hope that the weather will be better by next week.
