April 28, 2025

April

Clerical Work

Last Monday, we started scanning documents for surnames beginning with the letter M and were able to finish more than half of them. Today, we continued our clerical work. Throughout the day, we scanned documents under M, prepared and removed files not needed for scanning, and renamed the scanned files. Specifically, my task was to staple and check the scanned papers from the alpha list. I also compiled them by portion. We’re almost done with M, with just a few more boxes left to scan.

April 29, 2025

April

Coding

We found out that some information needed for transferee students hadn't been included in the system. My task today was to add text input fields for the date of transfer, the page numbers of attachments like transcripts of records from other schools, and pages for Bicol University records. This data will later be needed for generating the Official Transcript of Records for transferees.

April 30, 2025

April

Coding

This afternoon, the URO held their second meeting with all the registrars from different departments and campuses. One of the agenda items was an update on the system we’re developing—BUSAS. In the morning, we prepared the flow of our presentation and checked for any bugs or issues that might come up during the demo. Amir and Kent served as the main presenters of the system, while Viv, Dio, and I handled the system demonstration. Questions were answered, suggestions were noted, and both the meeting and our presentation were a success. As a bonus, we got burgers, fries, pineapple drinks, and fruits for our snack during the meeting.

May 1, 2025

May

Clerical Work

Today is Labor Day, but we still reported to work. The URO employees were working overtime, so we were allowed to come in as well. We chose to work even on a holiday because we’re short by one day if we want to complete our OJT by May 9. Since we were working during their overtime, our tasks were mostly clerical. We finished scanning the remaining files under letter M and started with surnames beginning with O.

I had the same task as Monday—I stapled, checked the scanned papers from the alpha list, and compiled the documents I stapled. My co-OJT members were in charge of renaming scanned files and preparing other documents for scanning.

Today was also the fiesta celebration in Namantao, where Ma'am Bam lives. All the employees, including us, went there for lunch. We rode in Sir Marc’s car since the place was quite far. We didn’t expect it, but it turned out to be a fun and memorable experience.

May 2, 2025

May

Coding

We just learned that the Ladderized Graduation Title doesn't follow the same format for former graduates. In the system we're developing, we hadn’t accounted for this and were only using the current format. So, we decided to add a feature to handle cases where the Ladderized Graduation Title is different. My task was to add another text field where users can input the alternative title. I also had to update the database by adding a new column through a migration.
